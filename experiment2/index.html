Experiment 2 - The Useless Canvas

Description

Tried to make a digital equivalent of the Useless Box.

Our circle friend's name is Redy. He prefers a clean canvas

Technical

Redy uses loadPixel() to scan the canvas for drawn lines

loadPixel creates an array that he can use to path towards

Redy then just deposits a white circle where he was, and then

scoots himself a little closer toward the black pixel.

Reflection

The project initially seemed simple enough, but it became more difficult as

I added more and more functionality to Redy. Another issue that came up

was I couldn't get it to work on my laptop, Redy kind of became a moron

and no matter what I tried, I couldn't get him to work. I thought it was

because of the processor power, but it turns out load pixel has issues

if the canvas isn't completely on the screen, and that is probably why

fixing the pathing logic didn't fix it at all. Unfortunately, it's too

late to fix that error. So it paths with loadpixel still
